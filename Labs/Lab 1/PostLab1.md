# Lab 1: Parametric CAD and Laser Cutting - Individual
## Description

Building upon the interlocking box that we made in class, I've designed a roof structure to transform the box into a house! The roof consists of two faces (2 different designs) which both have 3 inch bases and 1.396 inch heights.

### Faces

The first face is slotted 1 inch in from the top, while second face which is slotted 0.396 inches in from the bottom such that their combined dimensions when put together add up to the total height. Applying what we learned from the in-class portion of the lab: 1. Tolerances can be kept to a minimum as cardboard is a flexible material. 2. All dimensions were fully parameterized, calculating all of the dimensions based on one driven dimension which was the base length.
